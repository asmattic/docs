Anker USB 3.0 docking station¶

The Anker docking station uses USB 3.0 and is capable of Gigabit Ethernet with 6 USB ports. The drivers are maintained by Display Link and include Ubuntu.

FIDO Universal 2 Factor Authentication (U2F)¶

Yubikey is a popular vendor that makes USB authentication hardware keys using the FIDO U2F standard. The newest and most versitile as of this writing is the Yubikey 4 Series as it comes in multiple form factors including USB-A, a penny sized USB-A and USB-C. While the U2F standard is the main reason for these keys, the 4 series also supports the protocols below.

  • FIDO U2F
  • Secure Static Passwords
  • smart card (PIV)
  • Yubico OTP
  • Code Signing
  • OpenPGP
  • OATH-TOTP
  • OATH-HOTP
  • Challenge-Response
